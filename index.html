Open the World with Open Source

Workshop Organised by the Monash Business Analytics Team (WOMBAT)

October 22-23, Melbourne, Vic


About

The focus this year's workshop is on developments in open source that make the world around us more understandable. The purpose of this workshop is to bring together analysts from academia, industry and government to learn and discuss new open source tools for business analytics and data science. You should come if you are an analytics professional, or leading a team of analysts or a developer of new open source tools.

Stay tuned for more details! Attendance will be limited to 100 participants for day 2's workshop, and 20 participants for each of the 8 tutorials on day 1. If you would like to be notified when registration opens, please provide your email here.
